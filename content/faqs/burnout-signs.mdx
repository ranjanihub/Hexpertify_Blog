question: What are the warning signs of burnout in tech professionals?
answer: >
  Common signs include chronic exhaustion, decreased productivity, cynicism about work, difficulty concentrating, physical symptoms (headaches, insomnia), withdrawal from colleagues, and loss of enjoyment in previously liked activities. Early warning signs are feeling overwhelmed constantly, dreading work, and needing caffeine or other substances to function. If you notice these signs, it's crucial to take action immediately.
