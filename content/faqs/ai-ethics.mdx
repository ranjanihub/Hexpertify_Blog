question: What are the main ethical concerns with AI development?
answer: >
  Key ethical concerns include bias in AI systems, privacy violations, lack of transparency in decision-making, job displacement, autonomous weapons, deepfakes, and concentration of power. Responsible AI development requires diverse teams, transparent algorithms, fairness testing, privacy-preserving techniques, and adherence to ethical guidelines. Organizations should implement AI ethics boards and follow frameworks like the EU AI Act.
