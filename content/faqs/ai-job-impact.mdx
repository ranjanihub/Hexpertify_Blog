question: Will AI replace human jobs in the tech industry?
answer: >
  AI will transform jobs rather than simply replace them. While some routine tasks will be automated, AI creates new roles in AI development, ethics, training, and oversight. The key is to adapt by learning AI-related skills and focusing on uniquely human capabilities like creativity, emotional intelligence, and complex problem-solving. Tech professionals who embrace AI as a tool will thrive.
